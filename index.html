El Pollo Loco

A - Move Left
D - Move Right
Space - Jump
E - Throw Bottle
El Pollo Loco is a 2D Jump 'n' Run Game where you play our Mexican Hero Pepe.
Collect Coins and Bottles of Salsa Sauce.
Defeat Chicken by jumping on them or throwing a Bottle at them.
But don't waste all of your Bottles on the Chicken because the Endboss
is waiting for you at the End of the Level and jumping on him just makes him angry.
